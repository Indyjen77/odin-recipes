Vegan Peanut Butter Fudge

Ingredients

  • 1 cup vegan butter
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 cups powdered sugar

Description

Fudge. Easy as pie, but fudgier

Steps

  1. Place vegan butter and peanut butter into a microwave safe dish with a lid. Microwave for 2 minutes
  2. Bring it out to stir very well so that they are well combined. Then return to the microwave for another 2 minutes. If it starts to boil it is done.
  3. Add the vanilla and powdered sugar and mix in.
  4. Transfer to an 8x8" square parchment lined dish and smooth with a spoon
  5. Place into the fridge to set. Once it has set, lift the whole block of fudge out with the parchment paper and cut into small squares.
